This interlinear glossed text uses the standard Leipzig glossing rules.

The gloss was generated using lexical and morphological data from the Open Scriptures Bible project, which is under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International license.

The original Hebrew text is from a unicode/XML version of the Westminster Leningrad Codex (WLC), a digitization of the Leningrad Codex.

The definitions come from James Strong's "A Concise Dictionary of the Words in the Hebrew Bible with their Renderings in the King James Version" (1894), transliterated by the Open Scriptures Bible Project under CC BY-SA 4.0.

Hebrew text is displayed in the Ezra SIL font, based on the typography of the Biblia Hebraica Stuttgartensia (BHS).
